WorkAdventure FAQ

Hello and welcome to DevConf.CZ WorkAdventure map!

The platform is made to recreate the social experience of DevConf.CZ in a game-like environment. You can walk around and explore the place. You can meet people in the hallways and have a chat or join them by the tables in the library or the coffee rooms.

You can now enjoy the after-talk discussions, chatting and meeting new people in a new engaging way!

Q: How does this work?
A: You can move around pressing keys: W/A/S/D (or arrow keys), you can explore the map and most importantly you can connect with others!

Q: How do I connect with others? Walk up to them!
A: Every player has a circle around them, when you come into the circle a Jitsi call will start. The hallway calls are limited to 4 people.

Q: Is there a participant limit for the call?
A: Yes, in hallways, regular rooms and outside you can only talk to upto 4 people. Session rooms have a bigger capacity, they are primarily dedicated to discussions with speakers after they finish their talk.

Q: How can I connect with a speaker on the map?
A: If the speaker chooses to, they can come into Session rooms (classrooms with yellow chairs) and participate in a discussion. The Session rooms number is the same as in Hopin / sched.com. When the Jitsi starts, the page will split in half, WorkAdventure on the left side and the call on the right side.

Q: I want to view extra content on the map (schedule/info/photos), how can I do it?
A: Generally, you should walk close enough to the icons representing extra content (schedule / info / photos ). When the link loads, the page will split in half, WorkAdventure on the left side and the content on the right side.

Q: How can I access Fedora DevConf.CZ 2022 badge or Red Hat page?
A: Find the Fedora/DevConf space on the map badge on the map and claim your badge via displayed URL.

Q: How can I manage settings?
A: In the bottom right corner you have a small camera and microphone, use that to manage your audio/video while being on the map. In the top left corner you have a mug and a mail, the mug can be used to edit your profile, character, name and more! More about the mail icon below.

Q: How can I chat with others?
